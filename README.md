# Network-Ports-and-Service-Protocol
I have Curated list of **Major Networking Ports and Service that operate in them**.   

To prepare this, Comprehensive Research was carried out on de-facto sources like [Shodan](https://shodan.io/) , [IANA](https://www.iana.org/) , [APNIC](https://www.apnic.net/) , [ARIN](https://www.arin.net/),  [Cisco Talos](https://talosintelligence.com/) , [SANS](https://www.sans.org/) , [Hurricane Electric](http://he.net/) ,  [Virus Total](https://www.virustotal.com/gui/) , [Rapid 7](https://www.rapid7.com/), [Security Trails](https://securitytrails.com/) , [Hybrid Analysis](https://www.hybrid-analysis.com/) ,[IBM Security](https://www.ibm.com/security) ,  [Mitre](https://collaborate.mitre.org/attackics/index.php/Technique/T885)  ,   [Censys](https://censys.io/) . Some of the port below are Dedicated assigned as Malware because there is no specific services Operating in them and over the years several Malicious Programs were using these port. All these ports are supported by [Shodan](https://shodan.io/) and [Censys](https://censys.io/) for Result Filtering using the Dork **port:**<port number> . Also, this list comes handy in SSRF , Intranet Pivoting, Trouble Shooting , Port Scanning , ............ for Professionals and Student can use it for Educational Purposes .
